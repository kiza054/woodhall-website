About Us

1st Woodhall Scout Group is located at 1st Pudsey (Parish Church) Group Headquarters, Greenside, Pudsey, LS28 8LP and has been established since 1957. We’re part of the West Leeds District within the Central Yorkshire Scout County. Our necker colours are black with a white border.

Within 1st Woodhall we have active Beaver, Cub and Scout sections.

Scouting is a world wide value based institution. It welcomes all youths of all backgrounds and all religious beliefs, both male and female.

If you want to find out more, please just get in touch via the contact us page or find us at 1st Pudsey (Parish Church) Group Headquarters at the following times (during term time only):

Section Meeting Day Meeting Time
