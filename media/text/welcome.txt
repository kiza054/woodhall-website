Hello and welcome to the 1st Woodhall Scout Group website.

1st Woodhall Scout Group is located at St James the Great Church on Galloway Lane, Pudsey, Leeds (map here) and has been established since 1957.

Scouting is a world wide value based institution. 1st Woodhall is an active and friendly group and welcome boys and girls from all backgrounds and all religious beliefs to make new friends, get outdoors, learn exciting skills and most importanty have fun!
